An "infra company" in India refers to a company primarily involved in developing and constructing large-scale infrastructure projects like roads, bridges, airports, power plants, and metro rails across the country, playing a crucial role in India's economic growth by building and maintaining essential infrastructure needed for development.

An "infra company" in India refers to a company primarily involved in developing and constructing large-scale infrastructure projects like roads, bridges, airports, power plants, and metro rails across the country, playing a crucial role in India's economic growth by building and maintaining essential infrastructure needed for development;:

prominent examples include Larsen & Toubro (L&T), Tata Projects, Reliance Infrastructure, IRB Infrastructure Developers, and GMR Infrastructure, which often operate across different sectors within infrastructure development, managing complex projects from design to construction and sometimes even operations and maintenance.
